About

Ephraim Ziwoya

I am a biomedical engineer with a passion for software engineering. My mission is to tailor healthcare technology to meet the unique needs of individuals, improving their health and wellness through innovative solutions. With a strong background in both fields, I strive to bridge the gap between healthcare and technology, creating tools that are not only functional but also impactful.

This project was inspired by my own experiences and challenges in managing health and wellness. I realized the need for a comprehensive tool that could help individuals track their health metrics and make informed decisions. HealthTrack is my effort to fill this gap and provide a solution that benefits everyone.

This project is part of my Portfolio Project for Holberton School. You can check out the project repository on GitHub.
